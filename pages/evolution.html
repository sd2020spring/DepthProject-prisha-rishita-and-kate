project evolution
witness our process

this project was in no way a simple job. we went through many different iterations, many different revisions, and many different tests before we deemed it suitable for the public. scroll to see our history

version 1

this version of the game was a simple black background, with screenshotted images of our sprites. we could only move from side to side, and we had major problems in making the game aesthetically pleasing. it functioned at a very mvp level, and was the bare minimum of what we wanted to present.

version 2

this version of the game was a little better. it had a nicer background, and the sprites had the ability to jump to reach their objects. we started to draw out some of the sprites as well, so they looked a little more like game characters, and could move around much better.

version 3

this version was our final version. we drew out all the sprites, making them part of the game, and we added multiple platforms for the sprites to be on. the player could jump back and forth between the levels, to gain as many objects as possible, and we added a homescreen and an endscreen, to make the game look more like a game.
