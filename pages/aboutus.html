meet the ideation behind our game

this is the working game!

when we decided to do this game, the world was in a state of panic, hoarding food, toilet paper, and other household items. we wanted to incorporate that in somehow, so we decided to make the purpose of the game to gain as many of these household items as possible, while also "quarantining", by staying away from all the infected people. the big idea was to encourage players to live their lives safely, encouraging the concept of social distancing, to try to get this pandemic over as soon as possible.
